# Limitations and Future Work

## Limitations

### 1. Identical parallel machines only

The study focuses on identical parallel machines. Uniform and unrelated machines are outside the current scope.

### 2. Static deterministic assumptions

The model assumes all jobs are available at time zero and that processing times and due dates are known.

### 3. Performance degrades with more machines

The paper reports that increasing the number of machines can reduce the accuracy and robustness of the proposed GA.

### 4. Benchmark-based evaluation

The experiments are based on benchmark instances. Real production deployment would require validation using actual shop-floor data.

## Future work

Future research can include:

- Uniform and unrelated parallel machine scheduling
- Energy-aware green scheduling
- Uncertainty in processing times and demand
- Setup and maintenance time considerations
- Real-time rescheduling
- Integration with manufacturing execution systems
- Dashboard-based decision support
